Our Heart

From abundance,
we give freely

We are a family deeply grateful for the blessings we have received through the grace of Jesus Christ. We believe that those who are blessed are blessed for a reason: to be a blessing to others.

Our giving is rooted in a simple conviction: that levelling the playing field in education and healthcare is one of the most powerful things we can do for the next generation. We give in the hope that one day, those whose lives are changed will look back and pay it forward, creating an unbroken chain of generosity that outlasts us all.

Every dollar we commit is designed to be sustainable, impactful and measurable. Faithful stewardship means giving with intention, not just sentiment.

2024 One-off
Healthcare · One-off Donation

Children's Cancer Foundation

Children are blank sheets of paper with unlimited potential. The family has a deep soft spot for children, believing that illness should never be the thing that writes their story for them. This donation to Singapore's Children's Cancer Foundation was given in the hope that even a modest contribution could alleviate some of the financial burden on families at their most vulnerable, and give a sick child one less thing standing between them and the life they deserve.

2025 Endowed
Education · SMU

Cheung Family Scholarship at SMU

The Cheung family has deep roots at SMU. In 2025, as the university celebrated its Silver Jubilee, the family was honoured to plant a tree outside the School of Accountancy to mark the occasion. That tree, still growing on campus today, is more than a symbol of the anniversary. It is an image of what this scholarship stands for: something planted with intention, nourished with care, and left to grow for generations that follow. We look not only for academic excellence but for character, young people who will one day give back what they have received.

2026 Endowed
Education · NUS

Cheung Family Scholarship at NUS

The Cheung family spent time at NUS and carries a lasting regard for what the institution makes possible. This endowed scholarship continues that story, empowering students from financially disadvantaged backgrounds to level the playing field and access a world-class education on equal footing with their peers. Our hope is that each recipient graduates not only with a degree, but with a conviction to one day extend the same opportunity to someone else.
